Trekking In Nepal

Nepal is appropriate country for trekking due to its geographical structure of landcape. Lots of Hilly area and Himalayan area covered by Nepal Land Structure so Trekking in Nepal is popular among the Tourist. To increase the number of tourists on trekking field Trekking Agency Association Of Nepal (TAAN) has started to increase number of beautiful trekking route for upcoming season. There are many famous trekking route in Nepal like Manasalu Circuit Trek, Kanchenjunga Circuit Trek, Upper Mustang Trek, Dhaulagiri Circuit Trek, Annapurna Dhaulagiri Panorama Trek, Annapurna Circuit Trek, Rara Lake Trek, Annapurna Base Camp Trek, Makalu Base Camp Trek, Upper Dolpo Trek, Langtang Valley Trek, Everest Base Camp Trek.

Bhutan Tour

Shaibya Travel & Treks is very excited to offer Bhutan Tour which is an adventurous trip into the mystical land of Thunder Dragon, Bhutan. Tour is Bhutan is just not a travel rather it is a trip of lifetime where you can experience true spirits of Bhutan in terms of People, Place, religions, cultures and lifestyle. B is recognized for spiritualism, remote monasteries, trekking into wondrous Himalaya, etc as well as for its pure natural beauty. Our Bhutan Tour in Bhutan focuses on deep valleys and fortresses and Bhutan traditional cultures, customs and traditions which have still lived. The major highlights of this Tour in Bhutan are Taktsang Monastery, Changangkha Lhakhang, Chendebji Chorten, Trongsa Dzong, Punakha Dzong, Zilukha Nunnery,Tashichho Dzong and many more. Bhutan is quite a place full of opportunity, experiences and is quite a privilege.

Tibet Tour

Tibet Tour is a giant plateau bordering Sichuan, Yuannan, Qinghai and Xinjiang around and to the south with India, Nepal, Sikkim, Bhutan and Burma and Kashmir on the west. This naturally gifted place is not only beautiful in terms of nature but also very generous in cultures. It sits on the highest elevation of world at 4900 M which is why Tibet is considered as the “Roof of The World”. The main attractions of the Tibet Tour are Cultural heritage sites, more than 50 snow capped Peaks, high altitude trekking areas, rich culture with kind people and many more. Tibet Tour is perfect touring place with unique landscapes, people, joy, colours, rich cultural highlights and kind people living in hardship.
